    Section2: What Makes or Breaks a Trip: Visitor Experience Analysis

    Every destination wants happy visitors, but few know what actually creates satisfaction. We analyzed thousands of New Zealand traveler reviews to indicate the exact factors that turn good trips into unforgettable ones

    This experience dashboard shows the most common feedback, grouped by experience category.Nature activities and safety create the best travel memories (94% and 92% satisfaction), while poor amenities and safety concerns ruin more trips than anything else. The data shows a clear pattern: travelers value authentic experiences and feeling secure above luxury amenities.

    • Green bar represent positive travel experiences
    • Red bar represent negative travel experiences
    • Circle size represents word frequency - larger circles indicate words mentioned more often by tourists
    • Words are grouped into categories: Nature Activities, Local Attractions, Amenities, and Safety
    • Actionable Insights: For Travelers: Prioritize destinations with strong safety records and nature access. Budget less for fancy amenities, more for quality activities. For Destinations: Invest in safety infrastructure and nature experience quality: these drive satisfaction more than expensive facilities. Safety is make-or-break: 92% satisfaction when good, only 27% when bad: the biggest swing of any factor.

    Section3: Tourism Spending by Purpose: Australia's $48.9B Market

    Breakdown of tourism expenditure by travel purpose and category in Australia, 2024 (AUD $M). Hover to see detailed spending

    Holiday travelers drive the biggest spending (37% of total), followed by education tourists(26%). Accommodation and food dominate expenses across all trip types, while shopping varies dramatically by travel purpose. This pattern shows Australia successfully attracts both vacation and education markets, creating more stable tourism revenue.

    • Inner circle represents the total expenditure
    • Middle ring breaks down spending by travel purpose (Holiday, Education, Business, etc.)
    • Outer ring shows detailed expenditure categories within each purpose (Airfares, Accommodation, Shopping, etc.)
